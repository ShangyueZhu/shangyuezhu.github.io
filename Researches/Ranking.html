Video Ranking System


Research Description

This research is a spin-off of short video research, which aims to generate a ranked list from a few hundred candidate videos for personalized recommendations.

  • Purpose. Create a video ranking list based on web log information.
  • Challenge.Data preprocessing (feature extraction) for complex log files, overfitting for small datasets.
  • Data. The website log data records the browsing time of the user, clicks on the link, and the source content of the webpage contains video metadata such as duration and bit rate.
